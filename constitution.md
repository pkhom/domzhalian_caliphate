# 0. Preamble
0.1. We, the 4 prophets of Belaszomizem, with the intention of reviving the ancient faith, declare the creation of Domžalski Kalifat, a nation meant to gatherer the followers of the forgotten faith, unite them and return the ones lost glory to our god and our people.

# 1. Symbols
1.1. The name of the nation is "Domžalski Kalifat"(transliterated to English "Domzhalian Caliphate").\
1.2. The flag of Domžalski Kalifat is a 3:2(w:h) rectangle of color #640809 with two #000000 stripes and a #271c35 1,5:2,25(w:h) romb in the middle.

# 2. Territories
2.1. Domžalski Kalifat does not have defined territories, as its goal is to conquer as many as possible.

# 3. Religion
3.1. The official religion of Domžalski Kalifat is Belaszomizem.\
3.2. Every other religion except Belaszomizem is forbidden and persecuted.

# 4. Government 
4.1. Domžalski Kalifat is governed by the Council of Caliphs.\
4.2. A new caliph can be appointed only by the decision of the current council. If for some reason the council is unable to make a decision, a caliph can be appointed by a national vote, needing to get more than 65% of votes.\
4.3. A caliph is appointed for life and can only lose power by the unanimous decision of other caliphs.

# 5. Government system
5.1. Domžalski Kalifat is a federal monarchy.\
5.2. A caliph may request for separating part of the territories into a federal state ruled by the caliph. The decision must be unanimous and recognised by every caliph.\
5.3. Each federal state must respect this constitution.\
5.4. The council of caliphs may revoke any law from the federal state's constitution.

# 6. Economy
6.1. Domžalski Kalifat is a socialist nation with a goal to give everyone same opportunities to grow and reward everyone for the work they do for the nation.\
6.2. Official currency of Domžalski Kalifat is [in development].

# 7. Citizenship
7.1. Citizenship is granted to a person by a caliph by his own decision.\
7.2. The only requirement is being loyal to our religion and our nation.\
7.3. As a citizen, you may take part in the internal politics of the nation as well as be promoted to government roles.\
7.4. Ones citizenship may be revoked only by the Supreme Court's decision.

# 8. Court and laws
8.1. All laws are passed by the Council of Caliphs by a majority vote.\
8.2. All votes of the Council of Caliphs must be made public and confirmed by the Supreme Court before the decision takes effect.\
8.3. If the Caliphs are divided between more than 2 options and the winning option has less than 61% of votes, the losing 40% get a veto right if they unanimously agree on it.\
8.4. The Supreme Court has a veto right on all the Council's decisions.
